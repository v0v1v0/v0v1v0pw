Usage

data('saving')

Format

A data.frame with 100 observations on 7 variables:

  • sav: annual savings, $

  • inc: annual income, $

  • size: family size

  • educ: years educ, household head

  • age: age of household head

  • black: =1 if household head is black

  • cons: annual consumption, $
