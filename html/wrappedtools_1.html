wrappedtools: Useful Wrappers Around Commonly Used Functions

Description

The main functionalities of 'wrappedtools' are: adding backticks to variable names; rounding to desired precision with special case for p-values; selecting columns based on pattern and storing their position, name, and backticked name; computing and formatting of descriptive statistics (e.g. meanÂ±SD), comparing groups and creating publication-ready tables with descriptive statistics and p-values; creating specialized plots for correlation matrices. Functions were mainly written for my own daily work or teaching, but may be of use to others as well.
